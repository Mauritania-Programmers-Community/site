# Welcome to MPC

We are excited to announce the launch of **Mauritania Programmers Community (MPC)** - a vibrant community of developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts in Mauritania.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to empower Mauritanian developers through:

- **Education**: Workshops, webinars, and learning resources
- **Collaboration**: Connecting developers with shared interests
- **Opportunities**: Helping members find jobs and projects
- **Innovation**: Encouraging the development of local tech solutions

## Join Us

We invite all developers, regardless of skill level, to join our community. Whether you're just starting your coding journey or you're an experienced developer, there's a place for you in MPC.

Stay tuned for upcoming events, workshops, and more!
