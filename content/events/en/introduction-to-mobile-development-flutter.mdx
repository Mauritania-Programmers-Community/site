# Introduction to Mobile Development | Flutter

Last Sunday, our community hosted a beginner-friendly session focused on Mobile App Development, led by Mohamed Lemine Zeidane, Mobile Developer.

## What You Learned

During this session, participants explored:

- The history of mobile app development
- Native vs Cross-Platform development
- An introduction to Flutter (Google Framework)
- Flutter advantages (Hot Reload, Performance, One Codebase)
- The complete mobile app development lifecycle
- Flutter roadmap for beginners
- Real-world apps built with Flutter (local & global)
- Free learning resources and Flutter installation steps

## Who Should Attend

This session was designed to help absolute beginners understand where to start and how to build a solid foundation in mobile development.
